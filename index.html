Event planning focuses on logistics, budgeting venue selection, and vendor coordination to ensure smooth operations and timelines. Event design, on the other hand, emphasizes creating the event's aesthetic and experiential aspects, including decor, lighting, and theme development, to craft a visually appealing and immersive experience for attendees.

Event planning encompasses logistics venue selection, and vendor coordination to ensure smooth operations and timelines. Event design, on the other hand, emphasizes creating the event's aesthetic and experiential aspects, including decor, lighting, and theme development, to craft a visually appealing and immersive experience for attendees.

While event planning handles the practical details logistics, event design complements it by creating a cohesive visual and sensory experience. Together, they ensure that both the operational and aesthetic aspects of an event are well-coordinated for a successful and memorable occasion.

Event design is crucial for a wedding as it sets the mood and creates a memorable atmosphere. Thoughtful decor, lighting, and theme development can transform a venue, making the celebration unique and reflecting the couple's personality and style.
